JUL - AUG 2020・UX DESIGN・DESIGN SYSTEM

AUO UX/UI Internship

Work as the only designer on the team to reimagine work order dispatch system
across web and mobile for solar energy maintenance team.

My role in the project
  • Conduct user research for maintenance dispatch software system and present insights, design concepts and solutions to the team.
  • Collaborate with a team of developers to iterate wireframes and prototypes.
  • Unify and define design guidelines and components such as data table.
Type

8 week internship

Mentor

Miranda Chou

。 Project Goal 。 UX Design 。 UI Design 。 Graphic/Motion 。 Takeaways

Goal

Develop design solutions for 3 kinds of stakeholders.


  • UX Design for User: Redesign current system to provide better experiences.
  • UI Design for Developer: Develop a design system to improve efficiency in the future.
  • Graphic/Motion Design for Investor: Craft 4 designs for promotional purposes.




    • Design Outcome


    UX Design for User


    I conducted desktop research and user interviews to different end-users, and translated the findings into insights and requirements. Then I proposed several design concepts to solve pain points and reduce users' cognitive load.


    Example Concept: Assign and Schedule Work Orders

    HYPOTHESIS

    If dispatchers can assign the work orders while having the access to the schedule of executors at the same time, dispatchers can spend less time assigning orders and do not need to switch between tools.


    EXPECTATION

    If there are more work orders assigned through website instead of creating from mobile App, the hypothesis is true.

    Takeaways


  • One-Person UX Team: I learned how to gain trust among a team of engineers by taking every opportunity to provide design ideas and introduce design thinking methodology at the same time. (The support from my manager is crucial as well!)
  • Communication with Stakeholders: Working on an internal system requires gathering feedbacks from various stakeholders. Sometimes it is unpredictable when the stakeholders have availability to be interviewed. Hence, I learned to be prepared beforehand and be able to conduct user interviews when the time comes.
