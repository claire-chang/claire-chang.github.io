What is Microsoft Monetize?

Xandr Monetize is a platform that helps publishers make the most money from their ad spaces by streamlining how they manage, sell, and optimize ads across websites, apps, and other digital channels.
It combines tools for serving ads, analyzing performance, and connecting with advertisers to ensure publishers get the best value for every ad shown, while also providing insights to improve their overall strategy.
